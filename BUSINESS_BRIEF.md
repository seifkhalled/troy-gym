## <span style="color: #00F0FF">Overview</span>

**ACHILLES** is an interactive human anatomy explorer and AI-powered fitness coaching platform. Users navigate a high-fidelity body map to isolate individual muscles, study their function, view recommended exercises and stretches, watch related video demonstrations, and receive personalized AI coaching context. It bridges the gap between *knowing which muscle to train* and *understanding how to train it safely and effectively*.

---

## <span style="color: #00F0FF">Problem</span>

1. **<span style="color: #CCFF00">Muscle Knowledge Gap</span>** — Most gym-goers train by routine, not by anatomical understanding. They don't know *which* muscle they're working, *why* it matters, or *how* to target it properly.
2. **<span style="color: #CCFF00">Fragmented Information</span>** — Exercise form, muscle anatomy, recovery data, and coaching advice live in separate silos (YouTube, Instagram, medical atlases, random blog posts).
3. **<span style="color: #CCFF00">No Personalization</span>** — Generic workout apps don't account for individual muscle imbalances, recovery needs, or targeted weaknesses.

---

## <span style="color: #00F0FF">Solution</span>

ACHILLES is a single destination where users can:

| Action | Experience |
|---|---|
| <span style="color: #00F0FF">**Explore**</span> | Click any muscle on a lifelike body map to see its name, function, and region |
| <span style="color: #00F0FF">**Learn**</span> | Access AI-curated coaching context, common issues, and form tips per muscle |
| <span style="color: #00F0FF">**Train**</span> | Browse a curated library of exercises and stretches with video/media support |
| <span style="color: #00F0FF">**Recover**</span> | View recovery time and weekly volume recommendations for each muscle |
| <span style="color: #00F0FF">**Ask**</span> | Interact with an AI coach for personalized training advice |
